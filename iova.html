Company Overview

Iovance Biotherapeutics is a clinical-stage cell therapy company focused on developing tumor-infiltrating lymphocyte (TIL) therapies for solid tumors. The company's approach leverages autologous T cell therapy to harness the body's own immune system to fight cancer.
