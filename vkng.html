Company Overview

Vikings Therapeutics is a clinical-stage biopharmaceutical company focused on the development of novel oral therapies for metabolic and endocrine disorders. The company is leveraging its expertise in the GLP-1 and glucagon receptor agonist space.

Clinical Trial Data

VK2735 Phase 2a (Primary Endpoint)
Patient Population: 200 obese patients (BMI 30-50) without diabetes
Primary Endpoint: Weight loss from baseline to Week 12
Results: 18% mean weight loss (VK2735) vs. 4% (placebo) — p < 0.001
Safety: Generally well tolerated. Most common AE was nausea (mild to moderate, transient).
Phase 2b (Ongoing)
Status: Enrollment ongoing
Sites: 15+ clinical research centers
Patient Population: 300+ patients with obesity ± metabolic comorbidities
Duration: 24-week double-blind treatment period
