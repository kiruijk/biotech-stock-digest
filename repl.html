Critical Risk Factors

Regulatory Path Uncertainty
RP1 BLA is based on Phase 2 accelerated approval pathway. FDA may require Phase 3 confirmatory data before approval, delaying commercialization by 1–2 years.
CRITICAL RISK
Single-Asset Concentration
Near-term value is almost entirely driven by RP1. Any setback — clinical, regulatory, or commercial — has outsized impact on stock price.
CRITICAL RISK
Reimbursement & Pricing Pressure
Oncolytic therapy pricing will face scrutiny. Competing with established immunotherapy combinations (Keytruda + chemo) limits pricing power.
HIGH RISK
Cash Runway & Dilution Risk
Burn rate is substantial. If BLA filing or approval is delayed, additional capital raise likely needed. Dilution at current price would be punishing.
FINANCIAL RISK
Merck Keytruda Dependency
Key trial uses pembrolizumab combination. Any changes to Merck collaboration terms or Keytruda label could affect REPL's program.
MEDIUM RISK
Manufacturing Scalability
Viral vector manufacturing is complex. Commercial-scale production of RP1 requires validated CMO partnerships and consistent yield.
MEDIUM RISK
